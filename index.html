Biography

AI-enabled materials research for energy systems

My work focuses on AI-driven materials discovery for energy storage, especially solid-state sodium-sulfur batteries. I build workflows that connect literature extraction, structured data, machine learning screening, and first-principles validation.

Current: Research Fellow, NTU MSE, Singapore.

Education: Ph.D. Harbin Institute of Technology; M.Sc. Harbin Institute of Technology; B.Sc. South China University of Technology.

Interests: AI for materials discovery, battery materials, multiscale modeling, DFT, and scientific data workflows.
